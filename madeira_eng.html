When:

16 - 23th June 2018


Where we slept:

We stayed in Villa Sol - Prazeres and managed accommodation via Airbnb. We were very lucky, our beautiful huge 3 bedroom apartment with kitchen, living room, 2 bathrooms, large terrace around the apartment with barbecue costs for 7 nights only €210!


Car rental: ... yeah sh*t happens!

We have chosen the Guerin company. Price: 110 GBP / 8 days (Category C Basic Rental with Insurances, CDW, THP, ZC, Seat Ibiza 4 door or similar; Economy 4 Door Car; Manual; With A/C) - this company was at that time the cheapest one.

Driver must be the holder of CREDIT CARD you paid the rent (not DEBIT, even the "European debit" - European debit cards work in USA, Canada, UK and many other countries the same way as credit cards, but not in Madeira). If not, a lot of problems begin... This happened to us. The easiest way is to buy a second driver for €5 / day. Any changes in confirmed reservations are impossible (they will cancel your paid reservation and create a new one, more expensive).

During reservation the websites pointed out that we bought a good insurance policy and no additional insurance is needed, even the staff will try to convince us at the airport spot. And that's exactly what happened. Staff were pushing us to buy additional insurance (another € 120) and claimed we only had a basic policy. We needed a lot of self-control to politely refuse. After this the staff was quite upset and I think that was the reason why they gave us bad car. Small, slow and extremely weak Fiat Punto! After 30 km of driving, suddenly the pump light came on! We decided to risk and ignore it for that day.
The next day, we planned a trip by the airport and changed the car without any problems. (Of course, we said that the warning light came on 20 minutes ago). This time we have received Renault Clio.
By the way, Guerin staff was trying to force us to rent a more expensive diesel car. (Suddenly they could change contract? Interesting...). Absolute nonsense, Madeira is the very hilly and curvy. The longest straight area on the island is runway ...
